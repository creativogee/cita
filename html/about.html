About Cita

This simple web application takes an input of your citation (APA) as copied from google scholar and edits it to standard

Reasons you MAY not get desired result:
  • The citation is not copied from google scholar
  • The citation is not in APA format
  • The citation is just wierd, lol
PS:

You may still need to manually expand some abbriviations
e.g Bmj = British Medical Journal

Thank you and hope you enjoy using cita
